# Set up via Smartphone
_Wi-Fi connection and Google Calendar integration_


This guide describes how to set up the panel using a smartphone.
You can also configure the panel using a desktop application.


For the full setup guide and alternative methods, please refer to: 

https://github.com/Grovety/Meeting-Room/blob/main/Installation_and_Setup.md

## Step 1. Prepare Required Information

_Before starting the setup, make sure you have:
 - Your Wi-Fi network name (SSID) and password
 - Your Google Calendar ICS URL_


### How to copy the Google Calendar ICS URL


#### Open Google Calendar **in a web browser (in desktop mode),** as the mobile app does not allow you to copy the ICS link.

https://calendar.google.com/calendar/

<img width="174" height="166" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/84194e79-b381-4a8b-a9a6-83c45d072cd7" />




#### Follow the steps to obtain the ICS link for your calendar

Under My calendars, locate the calendar you want, (1) click the three dots **(⋮)** next to the calendar name, (2) Select **Settings and sharing**

## Step 2. Panel Setup
1.	Tap the gear icon in the top-left corner of the panel to open Settings

2.	Select Setup Portal
<img width="600"  alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5bdc4f99-842f-49df-bdef-d83453f76e02" />


3.	Connect your phone to the panel’s Wi-Fi network:
 - Either scan the QR code with your phone camera for automatic connection
 - Or manually select the network and enter the password
 <img width="600" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/32849c0c-d2e6-48a9-9980-b26df89e1b0f" />

 - Tap “Always Connect” when prompted
   
<img width="300" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5b123bbd-b45d-4cd6-b162-93d793f96e12" />



4.	While connected to the panel’s network:

Open the setup page by:
 - Scanning the second QR code, or
 - Entering the following address in your phone’s browser: **192.168.4.1**

   
<img width="600" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7b668885-ad52-4891-ba38-2aae1b80b739" />
   

5.	On the setup page: Go to **Nearby Wi-Fi Networks**


<img width="300" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f1000dfc-ccc9-4e34-a00c-326f95bd0726" />
 

6. Select your home Wi-Fi network, and enter the Wi-Fi password


<img width="300" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/350cd455-eebe-4ea6-b7b4-a7d3ee82724c" />


7.	In the Google Calendar ICS URL field:
Paste the previously copied calendar link


<img width="300" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/46784c9d-7631-412d-aeb2-b90434817684" />

 

8.	Tap **Save and Reboot**

   
<img width="300" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a2d64202-236a-40cb-8f0b-93995ea6c8b0" />
 

9.  After reboot:
 - The panel will connect to the selected Wi-Fi network
 - The Google Calendar will be synchronized
 - The device will return to normal operation mode
